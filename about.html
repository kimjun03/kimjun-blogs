about me.

I'm a third year BSIT student Gingoog City Colleges.

Meet a soul whose heart beats to the rhythm of strings, a melody weaver and storyteller whose fingers dance across the fretboard with effortless grace. Born of passion and nurtured by dedication, this individual finds solace in the embrace of six strings and the resonance of wood.

With each strum, they breathe life into melodies that speak volumes, painting vivid landscapes of emotion and expression. In the hands of this musician, every chord is a journey, every note a revelation, and every song a testament to the power of music to inspire, heal, and connect.
